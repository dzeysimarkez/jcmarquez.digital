ASEAN Digital Innovation Programme

The ASEAN Digital Innovation Programme (ADIP) is a joint initiative between the ASEAN Foundation and Microsoft that aims to create a generation of future-ready ASEAN youth.

January 2017 JC Marquez
ASEAN Digital Innovation Programme focuses on capacitating underserved youth on digital skills and computer science in seven ASEAN Member States.

In partnership with Microsoft and Empire Code Singapore, this ASEAN Programme targets to equip 46,000 underserved young people aged 15 to 35 with digital skills that will allow them to thrive in the industrial revolution.

The event highlighted the FutureReadyASEAN platform that includes responsive tech training on digital programming languages. The workshop's objective is to train 40 participants on doing and gender-responsive tech training.

Selected from more than 280 applicants, the trainer participants are expected to teach and re-echo their learnings to atleast 100 marginalized youth in their own communities.
